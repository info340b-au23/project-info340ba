INTERESTED IN RESEARCH?

Here's a student-advice guide on how to get your foot in the door and build valuable experience for internships and your major application process!

WHERE DO I FIND OPPORTUNITIES?

  • Always check the URP Database!

  • This is your go-to hub for all things research. Regularly checking the URP Database can help you discover a plethora of research opportunities tailored to your interests.

  • Sending out apps 3-6 times a quarter throughout the year, REGARDLESS of your skill level or experience, is a great way to get your foot in the door. Oftentimes, attending informational sessions or reachout to professors can be fortuitous.

  • Networking with your professors (even in introductory courses!) can be a great way to seek out opportunities in person. If you are applying to a STEM major, doing well in your classes is baseline, and using your knowledge to ask meaningful questions in class can be a good personal introduction.
